01 / The leak

Most owner-led businesses leak revenue in the same four places.

You are good at the work. The leak is everything happening around it, the calls you cannot pick up, the leads that go cold, the same answers typed by hand all day. Here is where it shows up.

01

After-hours calls and DMs missed

The customer who calls at 8pm does not leave a voicemail. They call the next name on the list. Every missed ring after closing is a job you never knew you lost.

02

Warm leads going cold before you reply

Someone reaches out ready to buy. By the time you finish the job in front of you and circle back hours later, they have already booked with somebody who answered first.

03

The same questions answered by hand all day

What do you charge, do you cover my area, when can you come out. You answer the same handful of questions a hundred times a week, and every one pulls you off the actual work.

04

Jumping between tools that do not talk

The booking app, the texts, the spreadsheet, the inbox. Nothing connects, so you become the glue, copying details from one screen to the next instead of running your business.
