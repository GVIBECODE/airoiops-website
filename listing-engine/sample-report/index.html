What we learned this month

What's working

  • Single-story angles outperform luxury by 3x in lead quality. Buyers responding tend to be 50+ planning aging-in-place.
  • LinkedIn punches above its weight. Lower click volume (142) but highest lead-to-click ratio at 16%. Real estate professionals and seller-side referrals.
  • Pool home content peaked May 12-19. Seasonal demand confirmed. Recycle is timing this well naturally.

What to consider

  • Pinterest underperforming. 29 clicks across 140 posts. Recommend swapping pin format for infographic style in next refresh.
  • RV Parking topic is light. Only 3 leads from 42 posts. Either niche is wrong for May or visual hook needs work. We'll re-test in June.
  • Add 2 more 55+ topic variations. Demand signal is clear. Henderson 55+ ranking #2 with limited inventory of related posts.

Your next seasonal refresh

5 to 10 fresh posts added to your queue. Summer pool homes, fall move-up timing, mid-year market data.
