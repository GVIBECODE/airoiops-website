The problem with the playbook every brokerage hands you

Every brokerage tells agents the same thing. "Post on social. Drive leads to your IDX site. We pay for the retargeting, the CRM, the AI voice. You feed the funnel."

Then they hand you a 12-page PDF.

  • Pick 20 search topics. Build 20 search URLs. Shorten each one through a branded domain.
  • Open Claude. Paste a 600-word prompt. Get back text. Move it into 24 separate spreadsheets, one per platform per week.
  • Open Canva. Build a master template. Resize for six aspect ratios. Drop your image into all 140 frames.
  • Convert images to MP4 for the video platforms. Upload everything to Publer's media library, sorted into folders.
  • Bulk-import 24 CSVs. Confirm every Facebook link converted to an uploaded image. Schedule the week.
  • Repeat for week 2, 3, 4. Then click recycle.

That's an afternoon if you know what you're doing. It's a week if you don't. And then you have to refresh it every quarter or the algorithm punishes you.

Most agents quit at step 4.

Frequently asked

What does the monthly fee actually pay for?
The $397 a month keeps your engine running: 28-day recycle automation across all 6 platforms, quarterly seasonal refresh of 5 to 10 fresh posts (included), revision support (we tweak captions, swap neighborhoods, update niches within 1 business day), compliance re-scans when state or NAR rules change, and a monthly performance summary. Without the monthly, the recycle eventually goes stale and your reach drops.
What accounts do I need to connect, and how?
You provide three things: your IDX site URL (no login, just the address), a Publer Business account ($25 to $50 a month, you sign up yourself, takes 5 minutes), and your social media accounts connected to that Publer (you connect each through Publer's own login flow, we never see your passwords). You then invite us as a limited team member inside your Publer. We can upload drafts and schedule. We can't publish without your approval, change your passwords, or touch your social accounts directly.
Do I need to build a new website or buy a new domain?
No. Your IDX site (Ylopo, kvCORE, BoomTown, etc.) is the destination. Every post drives traffic to your existing site, where your retargeting and AI calling already take over. Branded short links are included as part of the service: your posts use short URLs like aiv.gs/rp-sw-pool700 instead of long IDX URLs. We manage the short link service on our end, no setup or maintenance on your part. If you want your own fully custom branded domain (yourname.live), that is a $25 a year add-on you own outright.
Can two agents use the same engine to save money?
No. Every post we produce is uniquely branded to one agent: your license number on every compliance footer, your headshot baked into every image, your broker name in every caption, and your IDX search URL as the destination. If another agent tried to publish your posts as their own, that is license fraud and gets them suspended. The drafts also live inside your private Publer account, not on a shareable link. Each agent needs their own subscription.
Will this work with my IDX vendor?
Yes if your IDX returns saved-search URLs. We support Ylopo, kvCORE, BoomTown, Sierra Interactive, Real Geeks, Chime, and most custom builds. If your URL pattern is unusual, we test it on the intake call before charging you.
Are the posts actually compliant for my state?
Every post is scanned against your state's MLS rules, fair housing requirements, and broker disclosure rules before delivery. We currently support NV, CA, AZ, TX, FL, and CO at launch. New states added quarterly. You still own final approval before anything publishes.
What if I don't have a Publer account?
We set one up with you on the kickoff call. The Publer Business plan ($25 to $40 a month) is required and is not included in our pricing. It's the engine that makes the recycle work.
Do you guarantee leads or closings?
No. We guarantee 840 published posts per platform per year, branded and compliant, recycling forever, with quarterly refreshes. Lead generation depends on your market, your stack, and your follow-up. We deliver the content engine. You close the leads.
What if I cancel after month 3?
You keep everything we built. The 24 CSVs, the Canva template, the Publer schedule. We turn off the recycle automation and stop the quarterly refresh. The setup fee is non-refundable because the deliverable is yours.
How is this different from the playbook my brokerage already gave me?
Your brokerage gave you the recipe. We are the kitchen. You can absolutely run their playbook yourself if you have an afternoon and the patience to do 24 CSV imports. Most agents don't.
