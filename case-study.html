The Challenge

What was broken before.

A solo operator at $9k MRR with one employee, growing 30 percent year over year. The problems were not effort or skill. They were everywhere the operator could not be at once.

01

1,000+ leads buried in CRM, no follow-up system

Meta ads pushed leads into a form, the form fed CRM, and that was the end of the workflow. No drip, no SMS cadence, no segmentation by lead age. A year of paid lead-gen sitting cold.

02

Google Ads conversion metrics didn't match real revenue

The dashboard reported 167 conversions at a $54 CPA. When we mapped those conversions to actual paying recurring customers, the count was 8. Most of the rest were form-fill events double-counted as both leads and purchases. True customer-acquisition cost was $1,112.

03

Manual bookkeeping eating 3 hours per week

The CRM payments were not flowing into QuickBooks automatically. Receipts piled up in a phone camera roll. Monthly reconciliation was a manual export-and-match job.

04

Idle video backlog, no content cadence

Hours of before/after job footage sitting on phones and SD cards, never edited, never posted. Inconsistent presence on TikTok, Instagram, Google Business, YouTube. Zero compounding social proof from work the operator was already doing.

Forensic Audit · 8 Months of Paid Spend

$8,983 in past Google Ads, measured against real revenue.

Not a screenshot tour. We pulled change history, search terms, ad copy, location data, schedule data, conversion config, auction insights, and 7 months of billing PDFs. Then we mapped every reported conversion against the operator's actual paying-customer ledger.

Optimization opportunity surfaced

Between 51 and 62 percent of the 8-month $8,983 spend was identified as optimizable. That is roughly $4,500 to $5,500 of past spend that, with the structural changes the Assessment recommends, would have produced significantly more recurring revenue. The Upsell We Shipped

We didn't just recommend lead recovery. We built it.

The Assessment surfaced 1,000+ idle leads as the #1 ROI play. Then we built the upsell as a working production system, not a slide deck. Same playbook now ships as a productized build for any operator who wants it.

01

5-message stay-in-CRM cadence

Day 1, 3, 7, 14, 30 follow-up sequence covering the entire 1,000+ idle-lead backlog. Built on top of the operator's existing CRM and Make.com stack. No new platform, no new monthly fee, no migration of historical lead data.

02

Send windows tuned to real conversion data

We ran a separate analysis on 326 prior leads to find the highest-conversion send window. Friday at 9 AM PT delivers a 60 percent reply rate; Monday at 5 PM PT drops to 20 percent. Blast script enforces the good window, blocks the bad one automatically.

03

Inbound auto-triage in under 60 seconds

Replies classify into 9 intent categories on a 60-second polling loop. Four safe categories auto-respond in the operator's voice (wrong number, yes, how much, spam). The rest queue as drafts the operator approves with one tap. No more 25-50 manual replies a day.

04

Self-tuning baked in from day one

Every send and every reply lands in a unified log. Weekly auto-tune adjusts send windows from accumulated conversion data. The system gets better the longer it runs, without anyone touching it.
