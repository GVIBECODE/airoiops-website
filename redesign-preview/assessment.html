Inside the report

The four parts of the report.

Every Assessment lands as one 12-18 page custom document. Here is what is in it.

  1. Operations Audit

    Deep-dive on your tools, workflows, and data. We map every recurring task in your business and tag it for AI leverage.

  2. Quick Wins You Can Ship

    5-7 DIY recommendations ranked by ROI and effort. Tools you can subscribe to, integrations you can configure in an afternoon, prompts you can paste into ChatGPT today.

  3. Upsell Menu

    Optional priced builds for the bigger plays: Speed-to-Lead, Custom GPTs, Zapier automations. Pick what you want, skip what you don't. Every build is scoped before you commit, with a transparent floor price plus a percentage of your projected ROI.

  4. AI Roadmap

    The sequenced path from your first Quick Win to a fully AI-leveraged ops stack. Phased milestones, prioritized backlog, no fluff.
