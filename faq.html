How much does an AI assessment cost for a small business?

Our AI Business Assessment is $1,000 flat. That includes the operations audit, the custom 12-18 page AI Roadmap delivered in 48 hours, and a 20-minute walk-through call. No retainers, no surprise charges, no 90-day discovery phase.

How long does the AI assessment take?

48 hours from the time you complete the intake. You can fill the deep-dive intake on your own time or book a 45-minute discovery call. Either way, your custom roadmap lands in your inbox 48 hours later.

Do you only serve small businesses in Las Vegas?

We are based in Las Vegas and we work with small business owners across Las Vegas, Henderson, North Las Vegas, Summerlin, Paradise, Spring Valley, Enterprise, Centennial Hills, Boulder City, Mesquite, Pahrump, and Indian Springs. The entire assessment runs over email and one short call, so we also serve remote clients anywhere in the United States.

What is included in the AI Business Assessment?

An operations audit of your tools and workflows, 5-7 DIY Quick Wins you can ship yourself, a transparent upsell menu for the bigger builds (Speed-to-Lead AI agents, Custom GPTs, Zapier automations), and a sequenced AI roadmap with prioritized milestones. All in a 12-18 page custom report, plus a 20-minute walk-through call.

Will the AI tools work with the software I already use?

Yes. Every Quick Win is scoped against your actual stack (CRM, email, scheduling, billing, ads). Builds run inside the tools you already pay for, not on a new platform. You own every credential.

Do I need technical skills to ship the Quick Wins?

No. Quick Wins are picked so a non-technical operator can configure them in an afternoon. If you can paste a prompt into ChatGPT or click through a Zapier setup, you can ship them. The bigger builds with code or multi-step automation are the upsells, where we hand off the technical work.

How are the bigger AI builds priced?

Each build has a floor price ($1,500 for Zapier automation, $2,500 for a Custom GPT, $3,500 for a Speed-to-Lead AI agent, $4,500 for a process optimization). We charge the floor or 20% of projected first-year ROI, whichever is greater. The $1,000 Assessment quantifies your ROI in writing before any build contract, so the math is on paper before you commit.
